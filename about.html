About Amanda!

About Amanda

Hi there! My name is Amanda Trent and I am the founder of Amandas Homemade Pastries. This business has always been a lifetime dream of mine, and I encourage all aspiring bakers out there to chase their dreams!

I was born in Atlanta, Georgia to my mother and father who inherited my grandmother's pastry shoppe. I grew up learning how to bake all kinds of pastries with my parents as well as how to decorate, display, and upkeep bakery goods.

I was set to inherit our family business until in 2012, my parents closed down the shoppe and decided to pursue other goals. This was a major disapointment, however it motivated me even moreso to pursue this dream of mine and open my own bakery.

After years of saving up finances and advertising my business, Amanda's Homemade Pastries opened in September of 2019! Now, we are a team of 12 strong, motivated, and talented bakers and decoraters!

If you are interested in joining our team or learning more, please contact us on the home page of this site!

Our Mission

From the beginning, our mission has been to inspire bakers to pursue their pastry dreams.

We want to make tasty treats accessable to all peoples within our area.

Today, Amanda's Homemade Pastries has served over 300,000 customers and employed over 50 talented pastry chefs and decorators.

Our Ingredients

We purchase all of our plant-based ingredients from Green Garden, an eco-friendly plant farm that sources all of their crops locally.

All other products are sourced from local food markets.
